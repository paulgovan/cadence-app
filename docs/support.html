FAQ

Quick answers

How do I change the visual style or speed?

Open Adjust > Session & Visuals to change the cue, pacing speed, and related settings.

How does Apple Health work?

Enable Health in Adjust > Health & Data. Cadence can write completed sessions as Mindful Minutes.

How do I export or restore my data?

Open Adjust > Health & Data and use Export Backup or Import Backup.

Can I use Cadence without an account or internet connection?

Yes. Cadence does not require an account, and the core app works offline.
