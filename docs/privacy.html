Privacy

Cadence Privacy

Last updated: March 13, 2026. Cadence keeps your app data on your device unless you choose to export it or share it.

What stays on your device

  • Session history and progress
  • Calm check-ins and notes
  • Custom presets and settings
  • Reminder schedules

Cadence does not require an account.

Optional permissions

Apple Health and notifications are optional. You can turn them on or off at any time.

If enabled, Cadence can write completed sessions to Apple Health as Mindful Minutes and send local reminders on your device.

Backups and exports

Backup files and reports are created only when you ask for them and are saved wherever you choose.

Those files may include personal notes or session history, so store them with care.

Accounts, ads, and sharing

Cadence does not require sign-in, does not sell your personal data, and is designed to avoid unnecessary third-party data collection.

Your controls

  • Turn off permissions in the app or system settings
  • Export your data before changing devices
  • Delete the app to remove locally stored app data from that device

Contact

If you have privacy questions, email takeabreatherapp@gmail.com.
